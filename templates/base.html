This is the place to check out local events in Surabaya. Take a look at what is happening, or create your own event and share it with the community. You have to login to create an event, so if you haven't already registered you can do that here.

Not sure where to hold your event? You're welcome to hold your event in our very, own cafe, a friendly, relaxed place to hang out and chat.

If you want to hold a workshop or a larger gathering then please contact us about our dedicated space in the cafe. If you wish to book the space we can also offer you and your group discounted food and drink.
